Products

Built on open-source technologies, we offer three core platforms that form a complete autonomous driving ecosystem—from cloud-based development to edge deployment.

Cloud.Robo

A cloud-native platform designed to optimize development and operations, enabling efficient data processing, model training, and system management.

Amp.Robo

A scalable software platform based on AmpRobo's extensible robotic system, supporting modular development for diverse autonomous driving applications.

Edge.Robo

A reference platform integrating sensors, computing, and software tools, providing a complete solution for prototyping and deploying autonomous systems.
